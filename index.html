Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account and use FairBid, including:

  • Account information: Your name and email address for authentication
  • Job data: Job descriptions, estimates, contracts, and client information you enter
  • Photos: Job site photos and receipt images you upload
  • Audio data: Voice recordings used for speech-to-text transcription. Audio is processed immediately and not stored.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve FairBid, process transactions, send service-related communications, and respond to your requests.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Job data, settings, and uploaded files are stored on Supabase servers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third Party Services

FairBid uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • OpenAI: AI-powered estimate generation. Job descriptions are sent to OpenAI for processing.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple: Payment processing and authentication

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from within the app under Settings → Delete Account. Account deletion permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

FairBid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
