# FairBid Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

## Overview
FairBid ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application for tradespeople 
to generate estimates, quotes, and contracts. This privacy policy explains 
how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account we collect your email address and password 
to authenticate you.

### Business Information
We collect the business information you provide including company name, 
address, phone number, and logo for use in generating your quotes and 
invoices.

### Job Data
We store the job descriptions, estimates, line items, client information, 
and contracts you create within the app. This data is stored securely 
in our database and is only accessible by you.

### Voice Data
When you use the voice input feature, your speech is processed by 
Apple's Speech Recognition service. We do not store raw audio recordings. 
Only the transcribed text is stored as part of your job records.

### Photos
Photos you upload are stored securely in our cloud storage and are 
only accessible by you.

### Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage data including job types, trade categories, 
and general usage patterns to improve the app. This data cannot be used 
to identify you personally.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the FairBid service
- To generate estimates, quotes, contracts, and invoices on your behalf
- To process subscription payments via Apple's App Store
- To send AI-generated pricing estimates via OpenAI's API

## Third Party Services
FairBid uses the following third party services:

- **Apple App Store** — subscription billing
- **Supabase** — secure data storage and authentication
- **OpenAI** — AI-powered estimate and contract generation
- **RevenueCat** — subscription management
- **Apple Speech Recognition** — voice transcription
- **Apple EventKit** — calendar scheduling

## Data Security
Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. 
We use Supabase for data storage which provides row-level security 
ensuring only you can access your data.

## Data Retention
Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may 
delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy
FairBid is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly 
collect data from children.

## Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data 
at any time by contacting us.

## Changes
We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app 
constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
